    Quick Tips
    • 1 route = 1 GPIO + 1 target MAC. Reuse one GPIO across routes to fan out to multiple MACs.
    • With Buzzer enabled: E-STOP press triggers alarm beeps; first Wi-Fi connection after boot plays startup tone (passive buzzer uses PWM tone output).
